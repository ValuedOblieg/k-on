Welcome to the K-ON! Club

ティータイム (Houkago Tea Time)

Houkago Tea Time is the fictional band formed by the main characters of the anime K-ON!. Composed of Yui, Mio, Ritsu, Tsumugi, and later Azusa, the group performs catchy rock and pop songs with heartfelt lyrics. Their music blends energetic guitar riffs with sweet vocals and keyboard melodies. The band name reflects their love for relaxing with tea and sweets after school. Despite their lighthearted approach, the members grow in skill and unity over time. Houkago Tea Time represents friendship, youth, and the joy of making music together.

Meet the K-ON! Members

Yui Hirasawa

Lead guitarist and vocalist. Energetic and carefree, she brings fun to the club.

Mio Akiyama

Bassist with a shy personality. She's also the lyricist of the band.

Ritsu Tainaka

Drummer and club president. Bold, loud, and full of energy.

Tsumugi Kotobuki

Keyboardist with a rich background. Sweet, generous, and loves tea time!

Azusa Nakano

Serious and passionate about music. The voice of discipline in the club.

About K-ON!

K-ON! is a heartwarming anime about a group of high school girls who form a light music club. They spend their school years playing music, bonding through friendship, and enjoying tea and cakes. It's a story of youth, growth, and unforgettable memories.
